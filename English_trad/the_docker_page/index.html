More detailed description :

Guatemala and Brazil :

In Guatemala, the volcanic lands provide an ideal environment for the cultivation of high-quality coffee. Brazilian farms are vast, with impressive landscapes of green hills covered with coffee trees. Both countries place great emphasis on sustainable production and adopt environmentally friendly practices.

Bean species :

Blue Montain

The Blue Mountain is highly regarded for its exceptional smoothness. Its coffee beans are grown in the lush and misty slopes of the mountains, at up to 2,000 meters of altitude. This unique microclimate, combined with rich volcanic soil, contributes to the coffee's distinctively balanced taste.
