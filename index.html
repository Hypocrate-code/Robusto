The Battle Creek's coffee plantation had been the first one to be discovered by Columbo Robusto during his several trips around the world. He first found the Ethiopian one when he traveled across the whole Africa ; from Algeria to Madagascar, in 1979. Legend has it that Mr. Robusto arrived in a tiny village in southern Ethiopia, and was welcomed by a mother with her child in the entrance, who gave him one white flower each as a goodwill token.
